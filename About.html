Why is are the repairs "Free" to some costumers?

If the costumer has Full Covereage for the damaged vehicle, then they are eligble for a Free repair. All expenses will be paid on behalf of the insurance. Dent Master will also pay your Deductible.

How long do repairs usually take?

It depends on how the damage on the vehicle is. On average about 5 days.

Does Dent Master Provide Loner cars?

Yes.However, we prefer for the costumer to have Rental car insurance. We also require the following verification before we lend a vehicle:

  • Valid Drivers License
  • Sign a No-liability Waiver
  • Sign a Agreement Waiver

Do I need to bring a estimate before I make an appoinment?

No, Dent Master can do an estimate for you. Insurance companies are known for "Low Balling" the estimate to save money. We will make sure we estimate your vehicle for what it acctually costs to fix.

If you already have an estimate from someone else, then Dent Master will write a new one and will supplement any additional damage that the previous estemator mightive missed.
